Paris Apartment

This project was a complete redesign of a classic Parisian apartment. The goal was to create a minimalist sanctuary in the heart of France, blending modern aesthetics with the apartment's historic charm. We focused on a neutral color palette, natural materials, and custom-made furniture to create a serene and timeless space.

The living room features a large, comfortable sofa and a custom-built bookshelf that spans an entire wall. The kitchen was completely renovated with state-of-the-art appliances and custom cabinetry. The bedroom is a peaceful retreat, with a large window overlooking a quiet courtyard.
